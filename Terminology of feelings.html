Terminology of feelings

In this project, I explore the rich landscape of human emotions by uncovering new words for feelings we have all experienced but often struggled to name. This visual dictionary features 15 carefully chosen terms which are probably the most common.

Presented in the form of a nostalgic and engaging book, this work invites you to relive these emotions, creating a space for reflection and connection. Each page serves as a portal to both familiar and uncharted feelings, allowing us to articulate our experiences in ways we may not have considered before.

I believe that emotions—whether positive or negative—are the driving force behind our existence. They remind us of our humanity and the vibrant tapestry of life that surrounds us. By giving voice to these feelings, this project aims to foster a deeper understanding of ourselves and each other, celebrating the complexity of our emotional experiences as we navigate the journey of life.
